Context

Why We're Building This

RoofOS is Design Roofing's proprietary marketing platform — built in-house, owned outright, and designed to scale. We're proving it in Austin first, then taking it nationwide as we grow and acquire. The $143,000 we currently spend annually on outsourced marketing is the baseline we're replacing with infrastructure we control permanently.

What the Current Program Misses
  • Google LSA — not in the program. The #1 ROI channel for roofing in 2026. $40–$120 per verified lead, Google Guarantee badge, appears above all standard paid ads. Industry consensus: every roofer should run this first.
  • Speed-to-lead automation. First contractor to respond wins 40%+ of leads. The 5-minute window is critical — leads not contacted in under 2 minutes convert 80% less. No AI-powered instant response in the current contract.
  • Storm surge automation. No weather-triggered campaign activation. Every storm event requires manual coordination — a direct cost for a business where storm and insurance work is a core revenue line.
  • Commercial lead channels. Commercial roofing requires different channels: LinkedIn, bid platforms, GC partnerships. None are addressed in the current scope.
  • Data ownership. All performance data, contacts, and campaign history live inside the vendor's dashboard. No owned analytics layer, no export pipeline, no continuity if the contract ends.
Platform Features

What Gets Built

Six modules, one platform — covering every channel and function the business needs. Each is owned infrastructure, not a rented vendor relationship.

01

Lead Capture — Three Verticals

  • Dedicated landing page per vertical: residential re-roof, commercial, and storm/insurance
  • Lead forms with source tracking — every lead tagged to the campaign and channel that drove it
  • Owned call tracking — phone leads attributed correctly, numbers never locked to a vendor
  • Google LSA integration — the biggest gap in the current program, built in from day one
  • All form fills, calls, and LSA leads flow directly into AccuLynx automatically
  • 108+ service area pages across Central Texas and expanding markets
02

Campaign Engine

  • Build, launch, and monitor Google Ads, Meta Ads, and Google LSA from one dashboard — no agency access needed
  • No approval cycles — campaigns go live the same day a storm hits or a new promotion is needed
  • Pre-built campaign templates per vertical, ready to activate with minimal setup
  • Budget controls and real-time spend alerts so ad spend never runs unchecked
  • Negative keyword management, audience targeting, and lookalike audiences built in
  • Campaign performance history accumulates — every decision gets smarter over time
03

Content & Social

  • AI-generated ad copy across all three verticals, produced on demand — no copywriter required
  • Automated publishing to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok on a scheduled cadence
  • Google Business Profile post automation — 8+ posts per month, AI-generated and auto-published
  • SEO blog posts and service pages — 24+ per year, faster than any manual content program
  • Local content per market — city-specific landing pages and content auto-tailored by service area
  • Consistent brand voice maintained across every channel and every market, at scale
04

Automation & AI Agents

  • Speed-to-lead agent — responds to every new lead within 60 seconds via SMS and email, automatically, 24/7
  • Storm surge agent — monitors weather data by zip code, activates pre-built campaigns the moment an event is detected
  • Review request agent — fires a post-job review sequence when a job is closed in AccuLynx, no manual step
  • Lead router — assigns every inbound lead to the right rep by service type and geography instantly
  • Content scheduler — queues and publishes AI-generated content across all platforms on a set cadence
  • Campaign monitor — watches spend and performance, surfaces budget alerts and optimization flags
05

Analytics & Reporting

  • Key metrics visible on the main dashboard — CPL, conversion rate, active spend, leads by channel
  • Full analytics view with cross-channel attribution — see exactly what's producing leads and at what cost
  • Campaign history builds over time — every future decision is informed by real performance data
  • Automated monthly reports compiled and delivered to stakeholders without any manual work
  • All data owned — stored in our infrastructure, never locked inside a vendor dashboard
  • Built for multi-market from day one — Austin TX and WV in one unified view
06

Reputation & Reviews

  • Automated review requests post-job — SMS first, email follow-up — no manual outreach required
  • Google Business Profile management across all locations from a single dashboard
  • Monitor and respond to new reviews without logging in and out of multiple accounts
  • Review velocity tracking — 25+ reviews at 4.5 stars is the minimum to compete for LSA placement
  • Review volume directly impacts LSA rank, map pack placement, and organic conversion rate
  • One of the highest-leverage, lowest-cost automations in the entire platform
Future Phase

Multi-Brand White Label — The platform is architected from day one for multi-tenant operation. When acquisitions begin in 2027, each new company connects to the same infrastructure — their own branded instance, local identity preserved, no custom build required per acquisition.

The AI Advantage

AI at the Core

AI isn't a feature added to this platform — it's the operating layer that makes the whole thing possible at this scale without a large team. Six agents built on Anthropic Claude run autonomously across every module, handling the work that would otherwise require daily manual effort or additional headcount.

⚡
Speed-to-Lead Agent
Trigger — New lead captured

When a lead comes in — form fill, call, or LSA — the agent sends a personalized SMS and email response within 60 seconds. No manual step. Runs 24 hours a day across all three verticals.

First responder wins 40%+ of leads. Leads not contacted in under 2 minutes convert 80% less.
🌩
Storm Surge Agent
Trigger — Hail or storm event detected

Monitors weather and hail event data by zip code in real time. When an event fires, pre-built campaigns auto-activate, landing pages update, and ad budgets shift — no manual coordination at any step.

Campaigns live before competitors even know a storm hit. Critical for the storm and insurance revenue line.
★
Review Request Agent
Trigger — Job closed in AccuLynx

Reads job status from AccuLynx and fires a personalized review request sequence — SMS first, email follow-up — without any manual trigger. Timing and messaging are optimized per service type.

Review velocity is a primary LSA ranking factor. This builds it automatically, every job, every time.
→
Lead Router
Trigger — New lead assigned

Routes every inbound lead to the right sales rep based on service type and geography. No manual assignment, no leads sitting in a queue, no handoff delays between incoming lead and first contact.

Every lead gets to the right person immediately — consistent follow-up regardless of time of day.
◈
Content Scheduler
Trigger — Scheduled cadence or on-demand request

Claude generates content — ad copy, social posts, GBP updates, email campaigns, blog drafts — and the agent queues and publishes across all platforms automatically on schedule.

24+ blog posts/year, 8+ GBP posts/month, daily social across all platforms — zero manual publishing.
◎
Campaign Monitor
Trigger — Continuous performance watch

Watches campaign spend and performance across Google, Meta, and LSA. Surfaces budget overage alerts, underperformance flags, and optimization opportunities without requiring daily manual review.

Active campaign management at scale — catches problems and surfaces wins before they become costly decisions.

The Build Plan

90 Days to Live

Three focused phases, each with a clear output. The goal is a working platform in production within 90 days — live, generating real leads, and testable — with time to optimize before acquisitions begin in 2027.

Phase 1
Foundation
Days 1–30
  • Dashboard shell, authentication, and core navigation
  • Three vertical landing pages — residential, commercial, storm
  • AccuLynx CRM integration — lead handoff and status sync
  • Owned call tracking setup — SMS infrastructure and number provisioning
  • Google LSA account setup and integration
  • Basic lead capture and source attribution running
  • Social accounts connected — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok publishing from day one
  • AI content generation live — social posts, ad copy, and GBP updates begin immediately
Phase 2
Campaign & Automation
Days 31–60
  • Campaign management dashboard — Google Ads, Meta Ads, LSA
  • Email and SMS drip sequences per vertical
  • Speed-to-lead AI agent — 60-second response on all leads
  • Lead router — automatic rep assignment by service and area
  • Analytics dashboard — CPL, conversion, active spend
  • AI content generation workflow — ad copy, email, GBP posts
Phase 3
Full Automation + Go-Live

  • Storm surge agent — weather trigger to campaign activation
  • Review request automation — AccuLynx job close trigger
